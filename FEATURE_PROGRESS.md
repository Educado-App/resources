### Certificate Issuance System

#### Description:

The certificate issuance system is a feature that distributes certificates to users that shows their work/achievements. These certificates are given to both students and content creators. The students receive certificates when they complete a course, such that they have proof that they have completed a course. The content creators receive a certificate when they request one, such that they can prove they have created it.
